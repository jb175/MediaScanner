# Privacy Policy for Online Usage ## Information We Collect In addition to the local usage, MediaScanner allows you to create an account using your email and password. These credentials are used with Firebase Authentication to create your account. All data stored locally, including ROMs and scanned media, are synchronized with Firebase. ## Use of Information The email and password you provide are used exclusively for the purpose of creating and managing your account. The data synchronized with Firebase is used to provide you with access to your media data across devices and to allow you to share your data with other users. ## Data Sharing By default, only you can access your data using your credentials. However, within the app, you have the option to give access to your data to any other user by validating their requests from the Account page. This is the only circumstance under which your data would be shared with other users. ## Data Security We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of your data. Your password is stored in an encrypted format, and all data transmission to and from Firebase is secured. ## Changes to This Privacy Policy Any updates or changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted within the app. By continuing to use MediaScanner, you agree to accept any revisions to this policy. ## Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at jb.moufle.dev@gmail.com.
